Internship Program

QometricsTech Internship Program

Welcome to the QometricsTech Internship Program. These Terms and Conditions (“Intern Terms”) define the rules, responsibilities, and expectations for all participants. By applying for and participating in this internship program, you agree to comply with these terms.

1. Acceptance of Internship Offer

Upon receiving an internship offer letter, candidates are required to confirm their participation within the specified timeframe. Failure to respond may result in cancellation of the offer.

2. Internship Duration

The internship will be conducted for the duration specified in the offer letter. Interns are expected to actively participate throughout the entire internship period.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

Interns are expected to:

  • Complete assigned weekly tasks and projects.
  • Submit work within the specified deadlines.
  • Maintain professionalism and ethical conduct.
  • Participate in project reviews and assessments when required.

4. Code of Conduct

Interns must maintain:

  • Professional behavior.
  • Respectful communication.
  • Ethical work practices.
  • Honest submission of original work.

Any misconduct, plagiarism, or inappropriate behavior may result in immediate termination.

5. Confidentiality

Interns shall not disclose any confidential information, project materials, internal processes, or business information obtained during the internship without prior written permission from QometricsTech.

6. Performance Evaluation

Performance will be evaluated based on:

  • Quality of submitted work.
  • Timely completion of tasks.
  • Participation and consistency.
  • Professional conduct.

7. Intellectual Property

All projects, assignments, documents, code, designs, or other work created as part of the internship shall remain the property of QometricsTech unless otherwise specified.

8. Attendance and Participation

Interns are expected to:

  • Remain active throughout the internship.
  • Respond to communications within a reasonable time.
  • Complete assigned tasks before deadlines.

Failure to participate regularly may affect certification eligibility.

9. Communication

Interns must maintain regular communication through the official channels provided by QometricsTech.

10. Learning and Improvement

Interns are encouraged to:

  • Accept constructive feedback.
  • Improve their skills continuously.
  • Demonstrate commitment to learning.

11. Termination of Internship

QometricsTech reserves the right to terminate an internship if:

  • Terms and conditions are violated.
  • Submitted work is fraudulent or plagiarized.
  • Performance is consistently unsatisfactory.
  • Misconduct is observed.

12. Internship Certificate and Letter of Recommendation (LOR)

  • Internship Completion Certificates will be issued only after successful completion of required tasks.
  • Letter of Recommendation (LOR) will be awarded only to outstanding performers based on:
    • Task performance
    • Project quality
    • Professional conduct
    • Consistency throughout the internship

Issuance of an LOR is solely at the discretion of QometricsTech.

13. Stipend

Unless explicitly stated in the offer letter, this internship program is unpaid and does not provide any stipend, salary, or monetary compensation.

14. No Guarantee of Employment

Participation in this internship does not guarantee:

  • Full-time employment
  • Part-time employment
  • Freelance opportunities
  • Future job offers

15. Program Participation Fee

A nominal participation fee of ₹99 may be charged for administrative and operational purposes.

The fee may cover:

  • Documentation processing
  • Certificate generation and verification
  • Project evaluation
  • Mentor support and communication
  • Platform maintenance and operational expenses

The participation fee does not constitute payment for employment.

16. Compliance with Laws

The internship program shall operate in accordance with applicable laws and regulations of India.

17. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising from this internship program shall be governed by the laws of India. Both parties shall first attempt to resolve disputes through mutual discussion before pursuing legal remedies.

18. No Employer-Employee Relationship

Participation in this internship program does not establish an employer-employee relationship between the intern and QometricsTech.

Interns participate solely for educational, training, and skill-development purposes.

19. Refund Policy

  • The participation fee is generally non-refundable.
  • No refund shall be provided for:
    • Voluntary withdrawal
    • Failure to complete tasks
    • Violation of internship policies
    • Failure to meet certification requirements

Exceptional refund requests may be reviewed at the sole discretion of QometricsTech.

20. Acceptance and Consent

By enrolling in the QometricsTech Internship Program, you:

  • Confirm that you have read and understood these Terms and Conditions.
  • Agree to comply with all internship policies.
  • Accept the participation fee structure and program guidelines.

Your enrollment constitutes full acceptance of these terms.
